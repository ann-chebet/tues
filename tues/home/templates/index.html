About us.

Sigoro Academy, Eldama Ravine, Kenya


The Sigoro Academy is an ordinary public educational institute in Eldama Ravine, Kenya. This school is managed / sponsored by religious organization and offers Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) , which is controlled by the Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) under Ministry of Education.

  • Ordinary
  • Day Only Schooling
  • Boys and Girls (co-education [co-ed] / mixed-gender education.

Official Elementary School Hours


Based on Basic Education Regulations 2014 of Ministry of Education, the official public or private elementary school working hours are as follows:
Class hours: 08:00 A.M. to 03:30 P.M.
Game, club and co-curricular activity hours: 03:30 P.M. to 04:45 P.M.
